\kafkasection{Derive an expression for the distribution of electron speeds in a plasma at pressure $p_e$ and temperature $T_e$ if the expression nunber density is given by
\begin{equation}
    \frac{p_e}{kT_e}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\ab(\frac{m_e}{2\pi kT_e})^{3/2}e^{m_ev^2/2kT_e}\d v_x \d v_y \d v_z
\end{equation}
}
We can turn this into electron speeds by declaring $v^2 = v_X^2 + v_y^2 + v_z^2$. We then turn $\d v_x \d v_y \d v_z = v^2 \sin{\theta}\d v \d \theta\d \phi$.
When integrating over all velocity space $\sin\theta \d \theta \d \phi = 4\pi$.
