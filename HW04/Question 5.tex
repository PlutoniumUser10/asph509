\kafkasubsection{A 5 solar mass main sequence star is in circular orbit with a 5 solar mass compact object (black hole). 
An isolated 5 solar mass star has its radius increase from 2.7 to 7 $R_{\solar}$ during the main sequence phase. During the red giant phase it expands from 7 to 100 $R_{\solar}$, and during the AGB phase its radius increases from 100 to 1000 $R_{\solar}$.

What orbital period ranges will result in Roche Lobe overflow (onto the black hole) during each of the three phases? (main sequence, red giant, and AGB).
The radius of a sphere with the same volume as the Roche Lobe around mass M1 is given
by: $R_L/a = 0.49q^{2/3}/(0.60q^{2/3}+\ln(1+q^{1/3}))$}
