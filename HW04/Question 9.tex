\kafkasection{In what year did C. J. Bland first appear in an annual photo of the UCalgary Department of Physics
and Astronomy? Explain how you know this.}
C. J Bland first appeared in the annual PHAS department photos in 1968-69. I know this because I went to where the photos are displayed and scanned the named persons starting at the earliest photo until I found C.J Bland.
