What is the energy flux of photons exiting a small hole?

The energy flux is given by:
\begin{equation}
    F = \int_0^{\infty} F_\nu \d\nu
\end{equation}
Where we integrate the spectral radiance over the frequencies. This spectral radiance is given by the Planck law for a black body.
\begin{equation}
    F_\nu = u_\nu = \frac{8\pi h \nu^3}{c^3}\ab[\exp\ab(\frac{h\nu}{kT})-1]^{-1}
\end{equation}
